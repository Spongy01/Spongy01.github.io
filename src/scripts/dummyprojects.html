InterConnectX

Ongoing Collaboration Hackathon
A backend middleware application designed to power bi-directional integration between systems supporting REST and SOAP communication. InterConnectX handles data transformation, security, and protocol compatibility, eliminating the need for complex custom integrations. By acting as a bridge, it enables seamless communication and efficient data exchange between REST and SOAP systems, ensuring compatibility through data format transformation and mapping.

Have an idea or suggestion for improvement? Feel free to reach out or raise an issue on GitHub!
